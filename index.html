Intersection Chicago

Welcome to Intersection. Intersection is a non for profit organization built to engage and educate communities through social contact and raise awareness of social issues. Intersection develops and implements educational and social programs that encourage dialogue that inspire cooperation and community cohesion. Intersection seeks to inform, educate, and engage members of the public on the subjects of stigma and stereotypes and their relation to the quality of life of individuals within our society

Our site is currently finalizing documentation before its official launch.
